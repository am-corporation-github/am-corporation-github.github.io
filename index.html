About us

We strive to create transformative brands that advance revolutionary individuals, businesses, and communities worldwide.

A&M Corporation is a design and IT company based out of Mumbai, India. Our sole focus is to help businesses tell their stories, build their brand and create a lasting impression. To accomplish this, we use a wide range of tools and techniques like graphic design, color, composition, photography, animation, and illustration. Our team consists of skilled and experienced graphic designers, illustrators, editors, and animators who work closely with clients to develop a custom visual solution that meets their needs and communicates the intended message effectively. We will also provide guidance and support throughout the entire process, from concept development to implementation and post-project evaluation. We also help the client with any revisions or changes to the design if required. We are knowledgeable in the latest industry trends and technologies, ensuring that our work is up-to-date and of the highest quality.
